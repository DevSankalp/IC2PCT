Galgotias University

Conference Committee

The Conference Committee of ICCSAI-2023 aims to create an intellectually stimulating and rewarding experience for participants, fostering academic growth, networking opportunities, and the exchange of knowledge indiverse fields of study. This committee plays a pivotal role in ensuring the success and smooth execution of conferences across different academic disciplines. One of the primary objectives of the Galgotias University Conference Committee is to create a platform that fosters intellectual growth, encourages research dissemination, and promotes interdisciplinary interactions among participants. They meticulously plan the schedule, manage registrations, arrange accommodations, and oversee the technical aspects of the conference, ensuring that both presenters and attendees have a seamless and enriching experience.
